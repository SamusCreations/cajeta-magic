Meet the Cajeta Shapper 3000!

Cajeta Magic started with a dream: to make the best cajetas for everyone. To make this possible, we invented our own machine—the Cajeta Shapper 3000! This amazing machine shapes up to three cajetas at once, saving time and making every candy perfectly round and smooth. Thanks to our Shapper 3000, we can offer more flavors and special versions for all our customers.

Our Eco-Friendly Packaging

We care about the planet as much as we care about our sweets. That’s why all our packaging is made from biodegradable and recyclable materials like paper and cardboard. It’s safe, easy to carry, and leaves no waste behind — just sweet memories!
